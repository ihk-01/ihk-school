A fun fact about me!

It started out innocently enough. I was looking for a way to stay fit and wanted to try something new, so when a friend of mine invited me to come to an open house at her martial arts studio, I gladly accepted. That's where I was introduced to Krav Maga. Not only did I enjoy participating in this new sport, it got me in the best shape of my life, gave me a close, new circle of friends, and taught me how to defend myself. Four years later, I earned my blackbelt. It was one of the best decisions I've ever made.
